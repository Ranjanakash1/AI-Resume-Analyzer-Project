ATS Guide

What is ATS?

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software used by companies to scan, filter, and rank resumes before they reach HR or recruiters. It helps employers quickly find the most suitable candidates based on keywords, skills, and job requirements.

How to Make Your Resume ATS-Friendly

  • Use standard section headings like Skills, Experience, Education
  • Add keywords directly from the job description
  • Use simple fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman
  • Avoid images, icons, text boxes, and heavy graphics
  • Use bullet points for better readability
  • Save resume in PDF or DOCX format if required
  • Keep formatting clean and professional

Common ATS Mistakes

  • Using fancy templates with too many graphics
  • Missing important job-related keywords
  • Using unclear headings or unusual section names
  • Submitting scanned image resumes
  • Adding too much unnecessary information
