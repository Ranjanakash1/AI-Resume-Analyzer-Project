Resume Tips

Best Resume Writing Tips

  • Keep your resume short and clear (1–2 pages maximum)
  • Use strong action words like Developed, Managed, Created
  • Add job-specific keywords for better ATS performance
  • Highlight achievements instead of only responsibilities
  • Use simple formatting and avoid too many graphics
  • Include a professional summary at the top
  • Keep contact details updated and professional
  • Check grammar and spelling carefully before submitting

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using unprofessional email addresses
  • Adding unnecessary personal information
  • Too much text without proper sections
  • Using outdated skills or irrelevant experience
  • Submitting resume without proofreading
